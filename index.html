I decided not to follow, instead I went on a two month trip to Australia & New Zeeland on my own. That's something I'll never regret, I learnt so much meeting new people and cultures from all over the world and were forced to speak english all the time.

Back home in Sweden again I started to work with my old boss from Swedish Match at a company called Brammer. I stayed there between 2016-2018 until it was time to lose my job for the same reason a second time. Shortly after I moved on to Volvo when I felt it was time to do something else. Through my ice hockey playing I met a guy in 2019 who had recently started a "digital foodcourt" called Nordish Market with his brother focusing on sustainable food. For me it was the perfect chance to work with cooking but unfortunately they couldn't offer me a full time job at that moment in time but it was still a great experience.
